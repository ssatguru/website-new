Introduction

The "AddInScout" plugin provides information on other AddIns loaded in by SharpDevelop.

Installation

  • Download the 'AddInScout-x.x.x.zip' file

  • Extract 'AddInScout.addin' and 'sps.SharpCode.AddInScout.dll' files from it using WinZip or some such program.

  • Copy the files to SharpDevelop's AddIns folder.
    Lets say SharpDevelop has been installed in 'c:\Program Files\SharpDevelop'.
    You will then copy the files to 'c:\Program Files\SharpDevelop\Addins' folder

Getting Started

To display the Addin go to 'Main Menu > Tools > AddinScout'. This will bring up the AddInScout Window.

The window contains three views , one on the left hand side and two, one above the other, on the right hand side.
The left view, displays a tree which lists out all the AddIns loaded in by SharpDevelop.
The right top view, displays information on an AddIn.
The right bottom view, displays information on an AddIn Extension and all the Codons under that Extension.

Clicking a AddIn on the tree, will display information about that Addin, in the right top view and information about it's first Extension, in the right bottom view.
Expanding the AddIn node on the tree will list out, under that node, all of that AddIn's Extensions.
Clicking an Extension on the tree, will display information about that Extension, in the right bottom view.

Clicking the 'URL' on the right top view, will open th explorer browser and take you to that URL.
Clicking the 'File' on the right top view, will open the file inside SharpDevelop.
